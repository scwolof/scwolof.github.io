# Title
Port wine beef casserole

# Keywords
meat port~wine beef casserole

# Ingredients
* 400g beef (diced casserole meat)
* 1 packet smoked pancetta
* 100g shallots
* 2 garlic cloves
* 2 carrots
* 2 celery sticks
* 200ml port wine
* 150ml single cream
* 1 tsp mustard
* 2 tbsp lingonberry jam
* 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
* Salt, pepper, parsley and thyme

# Instructions
* Roughly chop up the carrots and celery sticks. Chop up the cloves of garlic, and quarter the shallots.
* Melt butter in a large pan. Add the garlic, shallots, beef and pancetta and fry until the beef is brown on all sides. Add the carrot and celery towards the end.
* Add the port wine and bring to a boil. Stir in the mustard, vinegar, lingonberries and herbs and spices.
* Add the cream. Let it simmer for around 1 hour.

# Note
Serve with boiled potatoes and/or some nice sourdough bread.
