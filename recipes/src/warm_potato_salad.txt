# Title
Warm potato salad

# Keywords
vegetarian side potato salad

# Image
warm_potato_salad.png

# Serves
2

# Ingredients
* 1 baking potato
* 1 courgette
* 1 onion
* Sour cream
* Oil, salt, pepper and parsley

# Instructions
* Cut the potato into small chunks (do not bother peeling)
* Put the potato chunks on a baking tray. Drizzle with oil and season with salt, pepper and parsley. 
* Bake the potato chunks at 200Â°C for 20-30min. Let them cool for 10min.
* While the potato is cooking, peel and roughly chop the onion. Cut the courgette into little quarter slices
* Fry the courgette and onion in oil.
* Mix the onion, courgette, potato and sour cream.

# Note
This potato salad goes very well with oven-baked salmon.
