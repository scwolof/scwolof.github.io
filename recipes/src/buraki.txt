# Title
Buraki

# Keywords
vegetarian beetroot side buraki babcia polish add

# Description
Polish warm beetroots

# Ingredients
* 2 punnets of beetroot (pre-cooked in malt vinegar) - CHECK WEIGHT
* 30g butter
* 2 tbsp sugar
* 1 tbsp flour
* Small carton single cream - CHECK AMOUNT

# Instructions
* Grate/shred the beetroot
* Mix all ingredients together over medium heat.
