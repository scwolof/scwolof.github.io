# Title
White cabbage salad

# Keywords
vegetarian side swedish cabbage salad

# Description
Traditional (?) Swedish pizza side dish

# Image
white_cabbage_salad.png

# Ingredients
* 500g white cabbage (~half a small head)
* 50ml vegetable oil
* 30ml white wine vinegar
* 20ml water
* Salt and plenty of black pepper

# Instructions
* Shred/chop the white cabbage
* Mix with other ingredients
* Add more vinegar, salt and pepper as needed.

# Note
Serve with lasagna or pizza.
