# Title
Swedish meatballs

# Keywords
meat swedish meatballs

# Image
swedish_meatballs.png

# Ingredients
Meatballs:
* 500g beef mince
* 500g pork mince
* 2 eggs
* 1 onion (grated)
* Garlic salt, black pepper and oregano
* 150ml milk
* 75ml bread crumbs

Broth:
* Flour
* 400ml beef stock
* 1 tbsp soy sauce
* (optional) some red wine

# Instructions
* Mix the milk and breadcrumbs in a large bowl, and let sit for approx 5min
* Add the rest of the meatball ingredients and mix everything together (get your hands in there!)
* Roll into small balls (dip your hands in water and place the balls on a wet cutting board)
* Fry the meatballs in butter. When they're cooked through, place them in a deep pan.
* Mix the flour with the fat in the frying pan. Add the rest of the broth ingredients. Pour the liquid over the meatballs in the deep pan and let them simmer for a bit.

# Note
Serve with (a) mashed potatoes, peas and lingonberry sauce, or (b) creamed macaroni.
