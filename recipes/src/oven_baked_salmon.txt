# Title
Oven-baked salmon

# Keywords
salmon fish

# Ingredients
* Salmon fillets
* Lemon juice
* Butter
* Salt, pepper and herbs (e.g. parsley or dill)

# Instructions
* Heat oven to 200Â°C (fan).
* Put the salmon fillets in an oven dish. Put several small knobs of butter on top of each fillet. 
* Sprinkle the fillets with salt, pepper and herbs. Squeeze a few drops of lemon juice over each fillet.
* Cover the oven dish with aluminium foil. Bake until done (~18min).
