# Title
Curry prawn pasta

# Keywords
seafood curry prawn pasta

# Serves
3

# Ingredients
* 300g prawns
* 1 pepper
* 1 small courgette
* 1 clove of garlic
* 1 tbsp Tomato purée
* 1 tbsp Curry powder
* 200ml cream
* 75ml white wine

# Instructions
* Cut up the garlic, pepper and courgette. Fry in butter together with curry powder and tomato purée.
* Add cream and wine. Simmer for 5-10min.
* Add prawns.

# Note
Serve with pasta.
