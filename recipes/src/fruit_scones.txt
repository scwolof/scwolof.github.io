# Title
Fruit scones

# Keywords
baking scones

# Description

# Image
fruit_scones.png

# Serves
Makes 8 scones.

# Ingredients
* 200g self-raising flour
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 50g block butter (softened)
* 125ml milk
* 15g sugar
* 75g dried fruit e.g. raisins

# Instructions
* Sieve the flour and baking powder into a large mixing bowl.
* Cut the butter into cubes and rub in the butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
* Add the sugar and dried fruit and mix.
* Make a 'well' in the mixture, pour in the milk a little at a time to make a soft but not sticky dough.
* Knead the dough on a floured surface.
* Roll out to a circle 3cm thick and cut rounds with a circular cookie cutter
* Brush with milk and bake at 200Â°C for 10-15min until risen and golden.

# Note
Serve with clotted cream and jam. Jam first!
