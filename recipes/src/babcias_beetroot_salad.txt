# Title
Babcia's beetroot salad

# Keywords
vegetarian side babcia polish beetroot salad

# Image
babcias_beetroot_salad.png

# Ingredients
* 1 onion
* 1 small carrot
* 1 peeled apple
* 8 small beetroots (pre-cooked in malt vinegar)
* 2 tsp sugar
* Salt, pepper

# Instructions
* Grate onion, carrot, apple and beetroots into a bowl
* Add sugar and season to taste
