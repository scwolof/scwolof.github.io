# Title
Chicken and chorizo paella

# Keywords
meat chicken chorizo prawns seafood paella

# Serves
4

# Image
chicken_chorizo_paella.png

# Ingredients
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1 onion
* 1 carrot
* Parsley
* Paprika
* 70g chorizo (Tesco finest)
* 2 chicken thighs
* 1 red pepper
* 1 tbsp tomato purée
* 1 stock cube
* 300g paella rice
* 100g frozen peas
* 200g seafood e.g. frozen cooked prawns
* 1 lemon

# Instructions
* Peel and finely slice the garlic, onion and carrot.
* Slice the chorizo and chop the chicken into small chunks.
* Add a fair amount of oil into a big pan.
* Fry the chorizo first, this really brings out the flavour of the chorizo. Add the paprika whilst the chorizo is cooking.
* Add the onion, carrot and chicken and fry for around 5 minutes.
* Deseed and chop the pepper, and add to the pan to cook for another 5 minutes.
* Add the rice and stir continuously so the rice soaks up the flavour.
* Dissolve the stock cube in enough water, and add to the pan.
* Add the tomato purée, parsley and lemon juice and season with salt and pepper.
* Add more paprika because paprika is gr8.
* Simmer until the rice is cooked, adding in the prawns and peas.
