How to get there

By car.

The A308 runs near the venue, and intersects with the M3 and the M25.


There is free guest parking at the venue. Please contact us if you wish to leave your car at the venue overnight. There is also paid parking available at Hampton Court train station and The Greens car park.


From central London.

There are regular trains from Waterloo station to Hampton Court station. The journey takes around 40 minutes. The venue is a 10 minute walk from Hampton Court station.

The trains also stop at Clapham Junction and Wimbledon.

The last train leaves Hampton Court station around midnight.

The venue can also be reached by taxi from the Richmond and Wimbledon underground stations.


From Heathrow.

It takes around 30 minutes to reach the venue by taxi (subject to traffic).


From Gatwick.

It takes around 40 minutes to reach the venue by taxi (subject to traffic).
